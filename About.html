About Us

Ez Gaming is a Esport organizer run by hardcore gaming enthusiasts. Ez Gaming has grown by leaps and bounds and is currently serving fans from all over Malaysia. Ez Gaming operates mostly online via the company official website, E-mails and Facebook. The organization is gaining momentum and volume in publicity, fans as well as international tournament.


Vision

Ez Gaming wants to play an important guiding role within five years by establishing, expanding and maintaining a successful, profitable organization within the leisure industry E-Sports.


Mission

Ez Gaming focuses on gaming teams in different game types. League of Legends, Counter Strike, Call of Duty, Dota, Heartstone and StarCraft are games which will be entered during 2019/2020. Ez Gaming also focuses equally on spreading content about E-Sports and all related topics, to serve our audience and our stakeholders.
