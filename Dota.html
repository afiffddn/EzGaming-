Dota 2

Invitation
The International features a series of tournaments before the event, known as the Dota Pro Circuit (DPC), with the top 12 ranking teams receiving direct invitations based on their final standings.

Format
  • 2 separate best-of 2 round robin groups consisting of nine teams each are played.
  • The remaining 16 teams move on to the double elimination main event.
  • Top 4 team advance to the upper bracket, bottom 4 advance to the lower bracket.
  • Every other round of both brackets is played in a best-of 3 series.
  • The Grand Finals between the winners of the upper and lower brackets in a best-of 5 series.
Prize Pool
The tournament's prize pool will be crowdfunded through a type of in-game battle pass, which raises money from players buying them to get exclusive in-game virtual goods and other bonuses.

Trophy
The Fall is a trophy that is awarded to the champions of DreamLeague. The reverse side of it is permanently engraved with the names of each player on the winning team.
