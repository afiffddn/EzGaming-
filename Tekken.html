Exciting Fight

The combo system in Tekken 7 retains some of the aspects of Tekken Revolution, in which it is no longer possible to bound a character during a combo. However, the bound state still exists, but a lot of moves that use to bound are being taken out.In order to compensate for the lack of bounds, many moves were given new combo dynamics in order to extend combos.

Tour Format
TEKKEN World Tour is an 8 month tournament series allowing players to qualify for the TEKKEN World Tour Finals. Players qualify by:

  • Winning the Last Chance Qualifier tournament at the Finals.
  • Placing in the Top 19 players on the Global Ranking Leaderboard.

Master/Master+
Master and Master+ tournaments are the tent-poles of the TEKKEN World Tour. Each of these tournaments are expected to draw international competition and awards Ranking Points to 1st through 25th Place. Evo is the special Master+ tournament, awarding increased Ranking Points to 1st through 49th place.

Challenger
Challenger tournaments take place in exciting locations around the world and award standard Ranking Points to 1st through 25th Place. Your best 3 Challenger tournament results count towards your Leaderboard ranking.

Dojo
Dojo tournaments award Ranking Points from 1st through 9th place depending on bracket size. There are 4 levels of Ranking Points awarded for Dojo tournaments and they begin at just 16 entrants! Your best 4 Dojo tournament results count towards your Leaderboard ranking.
